Lasagna

This is a lasagna, is really tasty and I like it a lot.

Ingredients

  • Lasanga noodles
  • 1 pund of beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Ricotta, mozzarella and cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Add salted water to a large pot. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Season a brown beef in a skillet with salt and pepper. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic.
  3. Combine with mozzarella, cheddar and ricotta. Stir well, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture.
  4. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes.

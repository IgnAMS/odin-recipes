Spaghetti

This is a plate of squash spaghetti, it is spaghetti, but inside a squash.

Ingredientes

  • 1 pund spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 tomato paste
  • 2 cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2.5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Boil a pot with salted water. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.
  2. Cook ground beef in a large skillet with onion until meat is brown. Return meat to pan with tomato paste, tomato soup and water. Cook stirring, until heated through and thickened, 5 to 15 minutes. Serve over cooked pasta.

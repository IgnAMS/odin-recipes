Baked Potato

This is a baked potato, it's a potato that is baked and you can fill with anything you want. In my case I like it with chedadar cheese and bacon.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Scrub the potato and prick it with a fork to prevent steam from building up.
  3. Bake for 1.5 hour.
  4. Bake the bacon in a silk until it's brown.
  5. Put the cheddar with milk in a silk, until it's melted.
  6. Cut the potato and put the bacon with the cheddar cheese.
